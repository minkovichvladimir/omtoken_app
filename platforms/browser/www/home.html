OUR GOAL

    Our goal is to create the ideal investment and trading environment that imposes lesser costs and restraints on transactions, creating a friction-less market. A user will use OMToken in his wallet to purchase a security instrument structured as cryptocurrency creating a permanent record of a digital transaction - immutable, secure and fast.
